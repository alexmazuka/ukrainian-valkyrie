About us

A mission born from war

Ukrainian Valkyrie was created in response to the greatest challenge faced by Ukrainian women after the start of the full-scale invasion: the loss of home, loved ones, support. We built a space where a woman receives comprehensive support — from first psychological aid to launching her own business.

Our approach is human-centered, confidential and free of charge for beneficiaries. We work with displaced women, wives of servicemen, female veterans, and women affected by the violence of war.
